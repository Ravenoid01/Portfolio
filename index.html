Froggo Jump!

Froggo Jump! is a casual game that aims to entertain users at any time of the day. Customize your frog and jump through various scenarios to help the frog "Toño" reunite with its friends. Within Froggo Jump!, you can find different mechanics and power-ups, which will make the game much more enjoyable – it even features local multiplayer! This way, you can compete against your friends, and with our leaderboard, you'll compete against everyone around the world.

Talkie Camp

Talkie Camp is a video game designed to enhance social interaction by immersing players in a collaborative environment. In this game, participants work together to construct, gather, cook, and safeguard their camp. Talkie Camp seeks to cultivate meaningful and lasting social connections by facilitating engagement between individuals, even when they are physically distant.
